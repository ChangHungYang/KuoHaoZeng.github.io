About me

I am a visiting student working with Dr. Juan Carlos Niebles at Stanford AI Lab. Before visiting, I was a graduated student supervised by Prof. Min Sun at National Tsing Hua University, majoring in Electrical Engineering. I am interesting in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, especially in Deep Learning.

My recent projects focus on video forecasting, visual-language understanding, and causality comprehension in the video. I've been researched on video highlight detection, video captioning, video title generation, and video question answering.
